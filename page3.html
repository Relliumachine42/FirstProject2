They made us in their image...

Heroes fade as cucumber threat dominates

During the first wave of the Qcumberean invasion, our world's heroes amassed to fight the then-unknown threat. A world united in terror, we watched as our heroes were viciously transformed into giant cucumbers.

Despite the years of Qcumberean rule, there is still little we know of their anatomy beyond its resemblence to a vegetable that is otherwise mostly harmless. Thus it is the leading theory that our greatest fighters have been transformed into more Qcumbereans rather than an oversized version of our earth vegetable.

This site is paid for by the Qcumberean Galactic Empire.
